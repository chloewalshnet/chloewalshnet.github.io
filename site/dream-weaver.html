Dream Weaver

A narrative puzzle adventure about shaping dreams

Timeline: 2022 - 2023
Team Size: 3 developers
Technologies: Unity, C#, Shader Graph
My Role: Lead Narrative Designer

Dream Weaver

Dream Weaver is a narrative-driven puzzle adventure game where you play as Luna, a talented young psychologist who discovers she has the ability to enter and reshape people's dreams.

Gameplay

In Dream Weaver, players navigate through surreal dreamscapes, each representing the subconscious mind of a different character. By manipulating dream objects and solving puzzles, you help characters process their traumas, fears, and desires.

Key features include: - Emotionally resonant storytelling - Dream manipulation mechanics - Unique visual style blending watercolor and geometric elements - Original soundtrack that adapts to player actions

Development Challenges

Creating Dream Weaver presented unique challenges in narrative design:

  1. Balancing puzzle difficulty with narrative flow
  2. Creating dreamscapes that were both visually stunning and meaningful
  3. Developing a dream manipulation system that felt intuitive yet powerful

My Contribution

As the lead narrative designer, I: - Crafted the overall story arc and character journeys - Designed dream puzzles that reflected each character's psychological state - Collaborated with artists to ensure visual elements supported the narrative - Wrote dialogue and dream journals that revealed character motivations

Dream Weaver has been featured at several indie game festivals and received praise for its innovative storytelling approach and emotional depth.
