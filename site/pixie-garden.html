Pixie Garden

A magical gardening simulation with fairy companions

Timeline: 2021 - 2022
Team Size: 4 developers
Technologies: Unreal Engine, Blueprint, C++
My Role: Game Designer & Programmer

Pixie Garden

Pixie Garden is a delightful gardening simulation game where players create and tend to magical gardens with the help of pixie companions. Each pixie has unique abilities and personalities that affect how plants grow and what magical properties they develop.

Game Design

The core loop of Pixie Garden involves: 1. Planting and nurturing magical seeds 2. Bonding with pixie companions 3. Harvesting magical plants with unique properties 4. Crafting potions and charms 5. Expanding your garden and attracting new pixies

Technical Implementation

As one of the programmers, I implemented: - A procedural plant growth system with visual stages - Weather and season effects on plant growth - Pixie AI behavior and personality traits - Inventory and crafting systems

Art Direction

Pixie Garden features a charming, hand-painted art style with: - Vibrant color palette inspired by fairy tales - Dynamic lighting that changes with time of day - Particle effects for magical interactions - Animated pixies with distinct visual personalities

Player Reception

The game has been particularly popular with younger players and those who enjoy relaxing gameplay experiences. Our community regularly shares screenshots of their garden creations, and we've incorporated many player suggestions into updates.

Since launch, we've added seasonal events, new pixie types, and expanded crafting options based on player feedback.
